Your Sky: Unable to Aim Telescope

Your request to aim the virtual telescope by clicking within the image could not be processed because your Web browser does not support "client-side image maps". When you upgrade your browser to one which includes this capability, you will be able to aim the telescope by clicking. Until then, you can aim by entering the aim point at which you wish to direct the telescope in the Right ascension and Declination fields in the telescope control panel. You can also display a variety of objects in the sky by choosing them from Your Sky's object catalogues.

Press your browser's Back button to return to the previous page.
